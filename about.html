About

I'm Guilherme Raul Cosentino Ferreira. I test autonomous vehicle perception systems at NVIDIA. Before that I spent three years at Stellantis building simulation pipelines for ADAS and writing HVAC embedded software. I started an MS in Computer Science specializing in AI at Georgia Tech, but paused it to join NVIDIA.

I grew up in Recife, Brazil. I live in Sunnyvale, California. Portuguese is my first language. I also speak English, Italian, and Spanish. I took courses in Mandarin Chinese and French, and I practice writing hiragana and katakana.

I'm into linguistics and natural language processing — how language works, how machines understand it, and what happens when those two things collide.
