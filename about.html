About

My name is Guilherme Cosentino Ferreira. I'm a simulation test engineer working on autonomous vehicles at NVIDIA. Before that I was at Stellantis building ADAS simulation pipelines and HVAC software. I'm currently pursuing an MSCS with an AI specialization at Georgia Tech.

I was born in Brazil and I live in Sunnyvale, California. I speak Portuguese, English, Italian, and some Spanish. I'm learning French, Japanese, and Mandarin.

This site

This is my personal corner of the internet. It has my resume, my writing, and eventually a knowledge base — a personal encyclopedia inspired by Piero Scaruffi's website, which I think is one of the greatest things on the internet.

The site uses zero cookies, zero JavaScript, and zero tracking. Pure HTML and CSS. It loads instantly, works everywhere, prints cleanly, and will still work in thirty years.
