About this site

A personal encyclopedia of culture, ideas, and knowledge. Inspired by Piero Scaruffi, designed to last centuries.

An encyclopedia is not a monument. It is a living conversation with the world.

What you'll find here

Opinionated reviews. Historical timelines. Curated lists. Original essays. Biographical notes on the thinkers, artists, and creators who shaped culture. No neutrality — these are my takes.

Ten sections cover the domains that interest me most: music, cinema, literature, science, philosophy, art, history, politics, technology, and travel.

Design principles

This site uses zero cookies, zero JavaScript, and zero tracking. It is pure HTML and CSS — a website the way websites were meant to be. It loads instantly, works everywhere, prints cleanly, and will still work in thirty years. The typography is chosen for beauty and readability. The palette is drawn from sand, stone, moss, and sky.

Who

Guilherme Cosentino Ferreira. Engineer by training, encyclopedist by temperament. Based in Brazil.

Colophon

Fonts: Cormorant Garamond (serif) and Outfit (sans). Colors inspired by the natural world. Hosting on GitHub Pages. Domain via Spaceship. No frameworks, no build tools, no dependencies. Just files.
